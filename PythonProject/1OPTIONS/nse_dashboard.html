This dashboard shows a rule-based read of current option-chain positioning (OI, change in OI, IV, PCR, max pain), plus a strategy library computed locally from live NSE premiums via your own nse_chain_server.py. Probability of Profit (POP) is a theoretical risk-neutral estimate from Black-Scholes using each strike's live implied volatility — it assumes lognormal returns and constant IV, not a guarantee or backtested win rate. Max profit/loss and breakevens for same-expiry strategies are exact given current premiums and will shift as premiums move; calendar/diagonal spread economics assume the far leg's IV stays constant until near-leg expiry. Margin for defined-risk spreads is approximated as max loss (the standard post-2021 SEBI spread-margin benefit); margin for undefined-risk strategies (short straddle/strangle, ratio backspreads) is a rough estimate only — always confirm with your broker's margin calculator before trading. This is informational only, not investment advice or a trade recommendation.
